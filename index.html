How It Works

Four steps from registration to on-chain ownership of your fixed-income token position.

01

Register

Create your investor account on the UNItoken platform. Provide basic personal and entity information to begin onboarding.

02

Verify Accreditation

Complete third-party accredited investor verification. Only qualified accredited investors may participate in UNItoken offerings.

03

Review & Sign

Access offering documents, legal summaries, smart contract disclosures, and compliance materials. Review transfer restrictions and custody arrangements.

04

Invest

Wire funds to escrow / SPV. Receive security tokens representing your preferred equity position, recorded on-chain with blockchain ledger transparency.
